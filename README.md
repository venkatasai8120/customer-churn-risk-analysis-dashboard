# Customer Churn & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing customer churn behavior and identifying high-risk customers using an interactive Power BI dashboard. The goal is to uncover key factors influencing churn and provide actionable insights to improve customer retention.


## Objectives
- Identify high-risk customers likely to churn
- Analyze customer behavior patterns and trends
- Understand key drivers of churn (contract type, tenure, services, etc.)
- Provide data-driven recommendations to improve retention

## Tools & Technologies
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
- Excel / CSV (Data Source)
- Data Cleaning & Transformation
- DAX (KPIs & Measures)

## Dashboard Features

###  Churn Dashboard
- Total Customers, Customers at Risk
- Monthly & Yearly Charges
- Demographic Analysis (Gender, Senior Citizen, Dependents)
- Subscription Services Analysis
- Contract Type & Payment Method Insights
- Internet Service Usage Distribution

###  Customer Risk Analysis Dashboard
- Churn Rate KPI (26.54%)
- Customer Distribution by Internet Service
- Churn Rate by Service Type
- Contract Type Analysis
- Tenure-Based Churn Trends
- Payment Method Impact on Churn
- Interactive Filters (Tenure, Contract, Internet Service)

##  Key Insights
- Customer churn rate is **27%**, with 1,869 out of 7,043 customers leaving
- Customers with **monthly contracts and lower tenure** are more likely to churn
- Customers without **dependents or partners** show higher churn rates
- **Fibre Optic users** have higher churn compared to other services
- **Electronic check** is the most common payment method among churned customers
- Lack of services like **Tech Support, Device Protection, and Online Security** increases churn likelihood

##  Recommendations
- Introduce **longer contract plans (3–6 months)** to improve retention
- Target **single customers** with personalized offers and discounts
- Include **value-added services** (Tech Support, Security, Device Protection) in standard packages
- Focus on **early customer engagement** to reduce churn risk
